Welcome to the World of Esperanto

Esperanto is an international language created to foster global understanding. Spoken by a community that spans continents, Esperanto is easy to learn and designed for everyone.

Why Learn Esperanto?

About Esperanto

Esperanto was developed in 1887 by Dr. L. L. Zamenhof to bridge cultural and linguistic divides. Its simple grammar, phonetic spelling, and diverse vocabulary make it an ideal second language for people worldwide.

Quick Facts About Esperanto

  • Creator: Dr. L. L. Zamenhof
  • Year Established: 1887
  • Speakers: Estimated to be in the millions globally
  • Purpose: To foster international understanding
  • Phonetic Language: Words are pronounced as they are written.
